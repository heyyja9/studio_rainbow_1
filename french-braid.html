French Braid Bracelet

Materials Needed:

● Rainbow loom

● Rainbow loom hook

● Light orange rubber bands

● Teal rubber bands

● C-clip

Step One

Choose two pegs that are right next to each other on the loom. Take a light orange rubber band, and twist it into a figure-eight shape across the two pegs.

Step Two

Place a teal rubber band straight across the two pegs (no twisting), followed by another light orange band on top, also placed straight across.

Step Three

Use your hook to grab the bottom light orange figure-eight band, and pull it up and over the other two bands on both sides.

Step Four

Add a teal rubber band straight across the two pegs.

Step Five

On the left peg, use your hook to pull the middle light orange band up and over the top.

Step Six

On the right peg, use your hook to pull the bottom teal band up and over the top.

Step Seven

Place a light orange rubber band straight across the two pegs.

Step Eight

This time, on the left peg, hook the bottom teal band over the top, and on the right peg, hook the middle teal band over the top.

Step Nine

Add another teal rubber band straight across the two pegs.

Step Ten

Repeat steps 4–9 to continue the pattern.
Keep alternating as follows:
Steps 5 and 6: left peg = middle, right peg = bottom
Step 8: left peg = bottom, right peg = middle

Tip: Whenever there are two bands of the same colour on one side, always hook the bottom band over. On the opposite side, hook the middle band of the same colour over instead. If the rubber bands on your bracelet start to look clumped together as you progress, gently tug on the end to help reshape and realign the design.

Step Eleven

When your bracelet reaches your desired length, you should have two rubber bands left on the pegs. Use your hook to grab the bottom band on both sides and pull it over the top, leaving one band on each peg.

Step Twelve

Secure one side of the remaining band with a C-clip, then repeat on the other side while carefully removing the bands from the pegs.

Step Thirteen

Find the loop at the start of your bracelet (the first light orange figure-eight band), and attach it to the same C-clip to close the bracelet.

Step Fourteen

Finished! Flaunt your awesome new french braid bracelet!
