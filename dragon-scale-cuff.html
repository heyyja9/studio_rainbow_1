Dragon Scale Cuff Bracelet

Materials Needed:

● Rainbow loom

● Rainbow loom hook

● Pink with blue stripes rubber bands

● Light green rubber bands

● C-clip

Step One

Choose five pairs of pegs that are next to each other (10 pegs total). Twist five striped pink rubber bands into figure-eight shapes, and place them across each pair of pegs, starting from the left peg and stretching to the right peg.

Step Two

Take four light green rubber bands and twist them into figure-eight shapes as well. Place these on the spaces in between the pairs of pegs (where there are no bands).

Step Three

Moving left to right across the pegs, use your hook to lift the striped pink bands and pull them over the light green bands, leaving the outermost left and right bands untouched.

Step Four

Place five more striped pink rubber bands straight across the same pegs as in step 1, but this time, don’t twist them, and just stretch them straight across each pair of pegs.

Step Five

Using your hook, pull all the bottom bands (both striped pink and light green) over the top bands on each peg.

Step Six

Place four more light green rubber bands across the pegs they were originally on, stretching them straight across each pair of pegs without twisting.

Step Seven

Use your hook to lift the bottom striped pink bands and pull them over the top, again leaving the outermost left and right bands untouched.

Step Eight

Repeat steps 4 to 7 with your alternating pattern in mind. Remember these tips: Always stretch new rubber bands straight across the pegs. Alternate between hooking over every bottom band except the outermost left and right ones, and hooking every single bottom band over.

Tip: The colour of the band you hook over is the same colour you'll replace across the pegs. If you hook over bands of both colours, replace the colour that had more bands hooked over. If the bracelet starts to clump between the rows of pegs, lift it up so it sits above them. To realign the bracelet and make the design visible, grab the five striped pink figure-eight bands at the bottom, and pull to stretch it out.

Step Nine

When your bracelet reaches the desired length, there should be a single band left on each peg. To prepare for attaching the C-clips, move each band from left to right onto the next peg beside it (except the last one, where it’s from right to left), so there are two bands on each peg.

Step Ten

Attach a C-clip to all five of these pegs with double bands. Once securely attached, carefully remove them from the pegs.

Step Eleven

Locate the five loops at the other end of your bracelet, and attach these loops to the same C-clips to close the bracelet.

Step Twelve

You’re done! Put on your dragon scale cuff bracelet and show off the stunning design!
