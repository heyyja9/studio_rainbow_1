Inverted Fishtail Bracelet

Materials Needed:

● Rainbow loom

● Rainbow loom hook

● White with black stripes rubber bands

● Magenta rubber bands

● Purple rubber bands

● C-clip

Step One

Choose two pegs that are right next to each other on the loom. Take a striped white rubber band, and twist it into a figure-eight shape across the two pegs.

Step Two

Place a magenta rubber band straight across the two pegs (no twisting), followed by a purple band on top, also placed straight across.

Step Three

Using your hook, grab the bottom striped white figure-eight band and pull it up and over the other two bands on both sides.

Step Four

Add another striped white rubber band straight across the two pegs.

Step Five

With your hook, lift the bottom magenta band over the other two bands, one side at a time.

Step Six

Place another magenta rubber band straight across the two pegs.

Step Seven

Use your hook to lift the bottom purple band over the other two bands, one side at a time.

Step Eight

Place another purple rubber band straight across the two pegs.

Step Nine

Pull back the middle magenta band with your hook, grab the bottom striped white band, and lift it over the other two bands, one side at a time.

Step Ten

Continue following the pattern in step 9 for the rest of the bracelet, pulling back the middle band and hooking the bottom band over. Tip: After pulling a band over, always place another band of the same colour straight across the two pegs. If the rubber bands on your bracelet start to look clumped together as you progress, gently tug on the end to help reshape and realign the design.

Step Eleven

When your bracelet reaches your desired length, you should have two rubber bands left on the pegs. Secure one side of the remaining bands with a C-clip, then repeat on the other side while carefully removing the bands from the pegs.

Step Twelve

Find the loop at the start of your bracelet (the first striped white figure-eight band), and attach it to the same C-clip to close the bracelet.

Step Thirteen

That's it! Wear your bracelet and show off the cool inverted fishtail design!
