Heart Link Bracelet

Materials Needed:

● Rainbow loom

● Rainbow loom hook

● White with black stripes rubber bands

● Pale pink rubber bands

● Light green rubber bands

● C-clip

Step One

Set up your Rainbow Loom so the middle column is one peg taller than the left and right columns. Then, choose two rows of pegs that are on top of each other (four pegs total).

Step Two

Take two striped white rubber bands. Twist the first into a figure-eight shape, and place it across the bottom two pegs. Then, place the second straight across the same two pegs. This will be the colour of the chain/links connecting the hearts.

Step Three

Use your hook to lift and hook the bottom striped white band over the top on both sides.

Step Four

To create the first heart, take two pale pink rubber bands: For the first band, start at the bottom left peg, twist it left into a figure-eight shape, and attach it to the top left peg. For the second band, start at the top right peg, twist it left into a figure-eight shape, and attach it to the bottom right peg.

Step Five

Place a light green rubber band straight across the top two pegs. This colour will form the inside of the hearts.

Step Six

Hook the left pale pink figure-eight band over the light green band, then repeat for the right pale pink band.

Step Seven

Hook the bottom striped white band on the left peg over the pale pink band. Then, do the same on the right peg.

Step Eight

Use your hook to grab and transfer the top light green rubber band to the bottom two pegs.

Step Nine

Add another striped white rubber band straight across the bottom two pegs.

Step Ten

Hook both the bottom pale pink and light green rubber bands over the striped white band on both sides.

Step Eleven

Repeat steps 4 to 10 with your intended pattern in mind. Important: for step 4, remember to pay attention to the band directions: The first band should always be twisted left, starting from the bottom left peg to the top left peg. The second band should also twist left, starting from the top right peg to the bottom right peg. If the rubber bands on your bracelet start to look clumped together as you progress, gently tug on the end to help reshape and realign the design. This will also help you start to see the shapes of the hearts.

Step Twelve

Once your bracelet reaches your desired length, you should now have one rubber band left across the bottom two pegs. Use your hook to transfer the band from the left peg over to the right one.

Step Thirteen

Attach a C-clip to the two bands on the right peg, then carefully pull them off the peg.

Step Fourteen

Find the loop at the start of your bracelet (the first striped white figure-eight band), and attach it to the same C-clip to close the bracelet.

Step Fifteen

Done! Put on and show off your lovely heart link bracelet!
