Three Row Frilly Braid Bracelet

Materials Needed:

● Rainbow loom

● Rainbow loom hook

● Yellow rubber bands

● Dark blue rubber bands

● Teal rubber bands

● C-clip

Step One

Position your loom so that the red arrow points away from you. The middle column should be one space lower than the left and right columns.

Step Two

Starting from the middle peg, stretch a yellow rubber band diagonally to the top right peg. From there, stretch another yellow band diagonally to the top left peg. Repeat this zigzag pattern with yellow bands all the way to the end of the loom.

Step Three

Return to the bottom middle peg. Lift the yellow band off this peg, leaving it loose on the top right peg. Then, take a dark blue rubber band and stretch it diagonally from the bottom middle peg to the top left peg. The dark blue band will hold the other bands together and keep the design intact. Return the previously removed yellow band to its original position, placing it on top of the dark blue band.

Step Four

Repeat step 2, but with the dark blue rubber bands instead.

Step Five

Rotate your loom so that the red arrow is now pointing towards you. Wrap a dark blue band twice around the bottom left peg to create a cap band. This will keep your bracelet secure, and provides a place for the C-clip to attach to later on.

Step Six

Use your hook to go under both the cap band and the regular dark blue band on the bottom left peg. Grab the bottom yellow band and hook it diagonally to the top right peg. Then, on the next peg, hook the bottom yellow band diagonally to the top left peg. Continue this zigzag hooking pattern until you reach the end of the loom.

Step Seven

Rotate your loom again so that the red arrow points away from you. Then, repeat step 2, but placing the teal rubber bands this time.

Step Eight

Rotate your loom so that the red arrow points towards you. Then, repeat step 6, but hooking the bottom dark blue bands diagonally forwards this time. Make sure you reach under all the bands to ensure you grab the correct dark blue band.

Step Nine

Return to the bottom left peg. Pull the teal band down slightly and position the cap band above it. Use your hook to go under the cap band, grab the teal band, and hook it diagonally to the top right peg. Then follow the same zigzag hooking pattern as before, hooking the teal bands forward this time.

Step Ten

When you reach the top middle peg, go under all the bands on this peg. Grab the bottom dark blue band and hook it diagonally to the bottom right peg.

Step Eleven

Attach a C-clip to the dark blue bands on the bottom right peg. Once securely attached, carefully remove the bands from the peg and pull the bracelet off the loom.

Step Twelve

Your bracelet may look a little weird when you first pull it off the loom. To fix it, pull the bracelet both vertically and horizontally, with the last stretch being vertical.

Step Thirteen

Connect the same C-clip from step 11 to the dark blue cap band at the other end of your bracelet to close it.

Step Fourteen

You’re done! Wear and flaunt your striking new three row frilly braid bracelet!
