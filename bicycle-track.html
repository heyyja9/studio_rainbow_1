Bicycle Track Bracelet

Materials Needed:

● Rainbow loom

● Rainbow loom hook

● Yellow rubber bands

● Light blue rubber bands

● Light pink rubber bands

● C-clip

Step One

Choose three pegs that are right next to each other on the loom. Take a yellow rubber band, and twist it into a figure-eight shape across the left and middle pegs. This first band colour will form one side of the track (or border) of the bracelet.

Step Two

Take a light blue rubber band and twist it into a figure-eight shape across the middle and right pegs.

Step Three

Add a yellow rubber band straight across the left and middle pegs.

Step Four

Use your hook to pull back the middle light blue band on the middle peg. Grab the bottom yellow rubber band, and pull it over the top.

Step Five

On the left peg, hook the bottom yellow band over the top.

Step Six

Place a light pink rubber band straight across the middle and right pegs.

Step Seven

This time, pull back the middle yellow band on the middle peg, then hook and pull the bottom light blue band over the top.

Step Eight

On the right peg, hook the bottom light blue band over the top.

Step Nine

Repeat steps 3-8 to continue the pattern.
Tip: The middle peg is always where you pull back the middle band, and hook the bottom band over, while the left and right pegs are where you hook the bottom band over. Always place a yellow band across the left and middle pegs, as it forms one side of the track. If the rubber bands on your bracelet start to look clumped together as you progress, gently tug on the end to help reshape and realign the design.

Step Ten

When your bracelet reaches your desired length, you should have two rubber bands left on the pegs: a top yellow band on the left and middle pegs, and the bottom last colour on the middle and right pegs.

Step Eleven

Hook the bottom band on the middle peg over the top.

Step Twelve

Move the band from the right peg to the middle peg, then do the same for the yellow band on the left peg.

Step Thirteen

Attach a C-clip to the three bands on the middle peg. Once it’s secure, carefully remove the bands from the pegs.

Step Fourteen

Find the loop at the start of your bracelet (the first yellow figure-eight band), and attach it to the same C-clip to close the bracelet.

Step Fifteen

You’re done! Wear and show off your amazing new bicycle track bracelet!
