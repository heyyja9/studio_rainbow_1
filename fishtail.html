Fishtail Bracelet

Materials Needed:

● Rainbow loom

● Rainbow loom hook

● Light green rubber bands

● Pink rubber bands

● C-clip

Step One

Choose two pegs that are right next to each other on the loom. Take a light green rubber band, and twist it into a figure-eight shape across the two pegs.

Step Two

Place a pink rubber band straight across the two pegs (no twisting), followed by another light green band on top, also placed straight across.

Step Three

Use your hook to grab the bottom light green figure-eight band, and pull it up and over the other two bands on both sides.

Step Four

Add a pink rubber band straight across the two pegs.

Step Five

Use your hook to lift the bottom pink band over the other two bands, one side at a time.

Step Six

Continue this process: add a new band straight across each time you pull the bottom band over. Alternate between light green and pink to create a striped fishtail pattern. If the rubber bands on your bracelet start to look clumped together, gently tug on the end to help reshape and realign the design.

Step Seven

When your bracelet reaches your desired length, you should have two rubber bands left on the pegs. Attach your C-clip to one side, then repeat on the other side while carefully removing the bands from the pegs.

Step Eight

Find the loop at the start of your bracelet (the first light green figure-eight band), and attach it to the same C-clip to close the bracelet.

Step Nine

You're done! Put on your bracelet and show off the cool fishtail design!
